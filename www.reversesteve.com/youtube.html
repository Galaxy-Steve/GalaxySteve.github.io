REVERSE STEVE YOUTUBE

INTRODUCTION

Lucas (born: January 26, 2006 [age 16]), better known online as Reverse Steve, is a French YouTuber and Photographer. He started his channel uploading solo using videos from his last channel (Maths & CO). Some of his most viewed videos include him making tutorials that helps people find an answer to their question. Lately Reverse Steve has been experimenting with YouTube Shorts and short Minecraft videos.

CONTENT

Reverse Steve mainly does tutorial videos on his YouTube channel. His tutorials can be about the "hacked" Minecraft client Toolbox Minecraft, technology, or just Minecraft in general (Minecraft Memes). Reverse Steve also makes PojavLauncher videos which is an app that let's you play Minecraft Java on your phone/tablet.
