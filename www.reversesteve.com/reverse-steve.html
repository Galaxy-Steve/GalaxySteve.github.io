Reverse Steve

INTRODUCTION

Lucas (born: January 26, 2006 [age 16]), better known online as Reverse Steve, is a French YouTuber, Photographer and Visual Artist. He started his channel uploading solo using videos from his last channel (Maths & CO). Some of his most viewed videos include him making tutorials that helps people find an answer to their question. Lately Reverse Steve has been experimenting with YouTube Shorts and short Minecraft videos.

Content

Reverse Steve mainly does tutorial videos on his YouTube channel. His tutorials can be about the hacked Minecraft client Toolbox Minecraft, technology, or just Minecraft in general. Reverse Steve has been making PojavLauncher videos lately but a new app called Hello Launcher has appeared and Reverse Steve makes videos on that topic, it is like PojavLauncher but with a bit more options. He is making Minecraft "memes" on PojavLauncher and Hello Launcher.

Personal Life

Reverse Steve was born on January 26, 2006. He is currently residing in Australia.
